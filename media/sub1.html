ABOUT

WHO WE ARE

New Balance is committed to doing business with Responsible Leadership, which means striving to have a positive impact in the communities in which we operate. We seek to ensure that the people who make our products - no matter where they are in the world - are treated with dignity and respect. We also believe in protecting our planet. We are constantly looking for ways to minimize and prevent negative environmental impacts in our operations and our global supply chain.

  • Get up-to-date information on New Balance’s latest projects and commitments within our community and around the world.

  • Manufactured in the US for over 75 years and representing a limited portion of our US sales, New Balance Made is a premium collection.

  • Movement is at the center of everything we do. We’re looking for team members who are committed to moving our brands, people and company forward.

MATERIALS AND DESIGN

Environmental Materials

Choosing more environmentally preferred fibers and materials is one of the best and most immediate ways we can reduce our impacts. We are focused on transitioning our three most highly used materials—polyester, leather and cotton - to environmentally preferred options.

Preferred Cotton

Cotton is among our top three textiles - a natural fiber that has been used for thousands of years. Cotton farming, however, requires vast amounts of water and chemicals. Preferred cotton includes cotton that is sourced as Better Cotton, recycled cotton, or organic cotton.

HUMAN RIGHTS

New Balance seeks to ensure that the people who make our products - no matter where they are in the world - are treated with dignity and respect. We believe that all people have a right to make their voices heard, earn a fair wage and work in a safe and healthy environment.

  • Worker Voice We believe in the importance of regular and constructive worker-management dialogue in the workplace

  • Fair Compensation We are committed to ensuring that the people who make our products are treated with dignity and respect.

  • Health & Safety We seek to invest in programs that benefit the health, safety and well-being of people in the workplace and beyond.

  • Labor Rights We enforce strict zero tolerance policies that prohibit child and forced labor as well as harassment and discrimination.
